Benefits of Adoption

Adopting animals from shelters helps give them a second chance at a loving home. Not only do you get a loving companion, but you also make room for other animals in need. Additionally, adoption fees are often less than the cost of purchasing animals from breeders or pet stores, and the fee typically includes vaccinations and neutering.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the adoption fee?

The adoption fee varies based on the animal's age, species, and any medical or behavioral needs. Typically, it covers vaccinations, spay/neuter surgery, and other basic care.

How long does the adoption process take?

The duration varies but can take anywhere from a day to a few weeks, depending on background checks, home visits, and other requirements.
